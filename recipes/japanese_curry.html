Japanese Curry

Description

Ingredients

  • 300g beef chuck roast (in cubes)
  • 2 potatos
  • 1/2 carrot
  • 1 onion
  • 1/2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 tsp grated garlic
  • 2 wedges of Japanese curry roux
  • 400mL of water

Directions

  1. Thinly slice the onions, and cut the potatos and carrots into bite sized pieces.
  2. Heat oil on meadium heat in a pot, and add the beef, garlic, and ginger.
  3. Once the beef is browned, add the remaining vegetables and cook for a few minutes allowing the onions to sweaat.
  4. Add the water, and continue cooking on medium low until the potatoes are fully cooked.
  5. Add the curry roux and cook for an additional 5 minutes until the roux is full dissolved and incorporated.
  6. Pour on a plate of warm Japanese rice to serve.
